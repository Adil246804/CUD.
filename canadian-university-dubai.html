Admissions

Your Journey
Starts Here

Joining CUD is straightforward. Our admissions team supports you through every step of the process, from initial enquiry to your first day on campus.

1
Choose Your Program
Browse our full portfolio of undergraduate and postgraduate programs and select the one aligned with your ambitions.
2
Submit Your Application
Complete the online application form and upload your academic transcripts, passport copy, and personal statement.
3
Receive Your Offer
Our admissions team reviews your application and issues a conditional or unconditional offer within 5 working days.
4
Confirm & Enrol
Accept your offer, pay your registration deposit, and complete your enrolment to secure your place.
Start Your Application
Fill in your details and an advisor will contact you within 24 hours.

Your information is 100% secure. We will never share your details.
